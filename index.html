How it all began

Our Story, Ng's POV

First Meeting · 2023

The first time I saw Joseph was in church sometime in late 2023. Our pastor introduced him as Solution and I wondered, "who names their child Solution?" I continued to notice him be involved in church and generally thought he was one "oversabi" guy with a touch of pride. I mean, the guy was EVERYWHERE something needed to be done in church. Subsequently I paid no mind to him and I reminded myself that I came to church to grow and plug in where I'd be able to serve, not to be a busy body.

First Conversation · 2024

Fast forward to January 22nd, 2024. J and I had our first conversation. He said hello on the church youth WhatsApp group but no one responded, except me. He slid into my DM to know who had responded and we began to get to know each other. By March 20th 2024, he asked me if I'd consider being in a relationship but also stated that he respected whatever situation I was currently in and awaited my response. I responded in the negative. I was not interested in a relationship at the time but I told him we could be build a friendship. And that's exactly what we did.

Just Friends · 2024–2025

In the course of our friendship, J checked in to see where I was with my decision about him. However, for all the negative responses he got from me (about 4) his commitment to our friendship never diminished. J has been keenly observant about everything that concerns me. He has been consistent in helping me grow and has been supportive of my career. He did not for once give me cause for concern as a friend, more so a friend of the opposite gender. I have always felt respected, honored and loved in our friendship and I believe these qualities have brought us to this moment.

The Proposal · 2025

In January 2025, Joseph asked me an important question: "Ng, should I keep waiting or should I search elsewhere?" At that moment I froze but I had no doubt that being in a relationship with him would be one of my best decisions, coupled with the positive responses from all the people I had sought counsel or asked about his personality. I agreed to be his woman on February 14th 2025 and it has been a smooth journey since.

To Forever · 2026

I look forward to blooming with this man, Joseph Ajayi and to being his help-meet under God.

How it all began

Our Story, Joseph's POV

Sunshine · 2023

It all started on a Sunday.
I was seated behind Dr. Ng and her mum in church. She was wrapped warmly in her mum’s arms as the service went on. They would nod in agreement with the Pastor, occasionally making quiet comments—like reminders of truths they already knew. And I found myself wondering: can this kind of love truly exist between a young lady and her mother?
That moment stayed with me.
Then came our Pastor’s 40th birthday celebration. She was there, serving tirelessly—making sure guests were comfortable and that everything went smoothly. It was clear: she had a heart for people, especially those she cared about.

The shot(s) · 2024 Not long after, I made a post on our church singles page. She was the only one who responded—and that became our first real point of connection.
From there, I started getting to know her more, drawing closer, and eventually, after careful thought and a bit of “background check” 😄, I began to make my intentions known. I wanted her to understand that I wasn’t just around—I had a goal.
But it wasn’t easy.
She turned me down four times.
At some point, she even told me she thought I was a proud person—something her friend also agreed with. Till today, I’m not entirely sure why I came across that way, but it was part of the journey.

To Forever · 2026

Then, after a whole year…
She said yes.
And here we are today. ❤️
